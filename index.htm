Introduction to Medical Missions in TJC

The Taiwan GA formed a group consisting of members who are practicing in the medical field. This group organized a 2-3 day training seminar and made visitation trips to the Philippines, where they provided free medical services to people while simultaneously preaching the gospel to them.

During the 2011 WDC (World Delegates Conference), the Taiwan GA suggested that the USGA consider having a similar initiative for visitations to countries in Central and South America, such as the Dominican Republic. As such, the USGA would like to survey the members in the United States to gauge the amount of interest in participating in medical missionary efforts, and see if coordinating medical missionary trips is feasible at this time.
