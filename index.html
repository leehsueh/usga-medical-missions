What Are Medical Missions?

The Taiwan GA has formed a group consisting of members who are practicing in the medical field. This group organized a 2-3 day training seminar and made visitation trips to the Philippines, where they provided free medical services to people while simultaneously preaching the gospel to them.

During the 2011 WDC (World Delegates Conference), the Taiwan GA suggested that the USGA consider doing something similar for countries in Central and South America, such as the Dominican Republic. As such, the USGA would like to survey the members in the United States and gauge if there is sufficient interest in participating in medical missionary efforts.

Are You Interested?

If you are someone who is practicing or training in the medical field, the USGA would like your input! Please take a few minutes to fill out this survey and express your interest in this ministry. By doing so, the USGA can evaluate the feasibility of pursuing medical missionary efforts in the near future.
